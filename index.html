Objective

You find yourself deep in a smelly, dank dungeon. The good news: you chose to be here. Legend tells of great riches buried deeper still, and 10 gold coins is enough to retire on.

Venture forth, collect coins, and stay alive. Each room is generated procedurally, so no two runs play out the same.

  • Win: Collect 10 coins.
  • Lose: HP reaches 0.

Controls

Desktop

  • W A S D — move
  • Shift — sprint
  • Space — attack (sword)
  • Enter — start game from title screen

Mobile

  • Left-thumb joystick — 8-way movement
  • Right-thumb Attack button
  • Right-thumb Sprint button
  • Rotate to landscape to begin

Combat

Press Space (desktop) or the Attack button (mobile) to swing your sword in the direction you're facing.

Stamina

Stamina gates sprinting and attacking. It regenerates automatically when you're walking or standing still.

Stamina bar (yellow)
  • Sprinting: drains stamina each frame.
  • Attacking: costs a fixed chunk per swing.
  • Regen (idle): faster than while moving.
  • Depleted: sprint and attack both lock out until stamina returns.

HP & Invulnerability

When you get struck, you lose HP and enter a brief window of invulnerability — your sprite flickers and the cyan bar ticks down.

HP (green/red)
Invulnerability window (cyan)

“Your GP or your HP!” — Black Mage

Enemies

Blobs wander each room until they detect you — then they'll give chase.

Rooms & Navigation

The dungeon is an infinite graph. Each room has 1–4 exits and is generated the moment you step through a doorway.

  • Exit counts are weighted — 2-exit rooms are the most common, 4-exit rooms the rarest.
  • Rooms you've visited are preserved. Walking back retains the map topology, but enemies scatter to new positions.
  • Coins spawn around the edges of a room — none directly in the middle, so you always have room to maneuver.

HUD

  • Top-right: coin counter (progress toward the goal of 10).
  • Status bars (bottom-left on desktop, top-left on mobile):
    • Green/red — HP
    • Cyan — invulnerability window
    • Yellow — stamina

Mobile customization

Mobile zoom defaults to 1.35×. If that feels cramped or too zoomed on your device, append ?zoom=<n> to the URL (any value from 0.5 to 5):

https://tfraczak.github.io/dungeon_crawler/?zoom=1.6

Your choice is remembered per device after the first load, so you only need to set it once.

About

Built in pure JavaScript and Canvas 2D — no frameworks, no runtime dependencies. Procedurally generated rooms, arc-based melee, and a web-audio sound engine rendered from scratch.
